Latitude vs Humidity

Here we find that there is a slight difference in humidity depending on the latitude - there is more occurances of low humidity as you go further away from the equator. During the colder months like winter, there is also an increase in low humidity days.

It's a very slight corralation as further findings show that the R-squared value is .0153 which means a high variability between the datapoints and the regression line.
